MAJOR DAMS AND WATER BODIES

KERWA DAM
=========

Overview:
Kerwa Dam is one of the major water sources for Bhopal and a popular picnic spot. Located on the Kerwa River, it offers scenic beauty and recreational activities for visitors.

Location:
- Distance: 15 km from Bhopal city center
- Direction: South-east of Bhopal
- River: Built on Kerwa River
- Accessibility: Well-connected by road

Dam Specifications:
- Type: Earth-fill dam
- Height: 45 meters
- Length: 1,200 meters
- Capacity: Water supply for Bhopal city
- Built: 1980s for water supply and irrigation

Recreational Activities:

**Water Sports:**
- Boating: Pedal boats and row boats available
- Fishing: Popular spot for angling enthusiasts
- Swimming: Designated safe areas (seasonal)
- Water Photography: Scenic water body shots

**Picnic Facilities:**
- Family Areas: Designated picnic spots with seating
- Food Stalls: Local snacks and refreshments
- Parking: Adequate parking space for vehicles
- Rest Rooms: Basic facilities available

**Nature Activities:**
- Bird Watching: Various water birds and migratory species
- Sunset Views: Beautiful sunset photography location
- Nature Walks: Walking paths around the dam area
- Children's Play: Safe areas for kids to play

Visiting Information:
- Best Time: October to March (pleasant weather)
- Timings: 6:00 AM - 6:00 PM
- Entry Fee: Nominal charges for vehicle parking
- Crowds: Weekends and holidays are busier
- Safety: Stay in designated areas near water

Facilities:
- Parking: Car and bus parking available
- Food: Local vendors and small restaurants
- Toilets: Basic restroom facilities
- First Aid: Basic medical facility during peak hours
- Security: Local police patrol for safety

Transportation:
- Private Vehicle: Most convenient option
- Public Bus: Limited service available
- Auto-rickshaw: Available but expensive for full trip
- Taxi: Hire for round trip

KALIASOT DAM
============

Overview:
Kaliasot Dam is another important water reservoir serving Bhopal. It's less commercialized than Kerwa Dam, offering a more serene and natural environment for visitors.

Location:
- Distance: 25 km from Bhopal city center
- Direction: North-east of Bhopal
- River: Built on Kaliasot River
- Surroundings: Hills and forest area

Features:
- Scenic Beauty: Surrounded by hills and greenery
- Peaceful Environment: Less crowded than other spots
- Wildlife: Occasional wildlife sightings
- Photography: Excellent for nature photography
- Trekking: Small trails around the dam area

Activities:
- Nature Photography: Landscape and wildlife photography
- Quiet Picnics: Peaceful family outings
- Bird Watching: Various bird species
- Fishing: Popular among local fishing enthusiasts
- Hiking: Short treks in surrounding hills

Visiting Tips:
- Bring Food: Limited food options available
- Early Morning: Best time for bird watching and photography
- Weather Check: Avoid during heavy monsoon
- Group Safety: Visit in groups for safety
- Leave No Trace: Keep the environment clean

MAJOR GARDENS AND PARKS

VAN VIHAR NATIONAL PARK
=======================

Overview:
Van Vihar National Park is unique as it's located within the city limits of Bhopal. It's both a national park and an urban green space, home to various wild animals in a natural setting.

Location:
- Address: Shamla Hills, Bhopal
- Area: 4.45 square kilometers
- Adjacent: Upper Lake on one side
- Established: 1979

Wildlife:

**Animals:**
- Tigers: In enclosed areas for safety
- Lions: Separate enclosures
- Leopards: Natural habitat within park
- Deer: Free-roaming spotted deer and sambhar
- Birds: Over 200 bird species
- Reptiles: Various snakes and lizards

**Bird Species:**
- Peacocks: National bird in natural habitat
- Eagles: Various species of eagles and hawks
- Water Birds: Due to proximity to Upper Lake
- Migratory Birds: Seasonal visitors
- Local Birds: Crows, parrots, kingfishers

Zones:

**Carnivore Section:**
- Big cats in secure enclosures
- Viewing areas for safe observation
- Feeding times for visitor experience
- Educational information boards

**Herbivore Section:**
- Free-roaming deer and antelopes
- Natural grazing areas
- Water bodies for animals
- Mixed species interaction

**Avian Section:**
- Bird watching areas
- Water bird zones near lake
- Forest birds in tree cover
- Seasonal migration spots

Facilities:
- Entry Gates: Multiple entry and exit points
- Paths: Well-maintained walking and cycling tracks
- Observation Points: Wildlife viewing areas
- Information Centers: Educational displays
- Guides: Nature interpretation services

Visiting Information:
- Timings: 6:30 AM - 6:30 PM (Summer), 7:00 AM - 5:30 PM (Winter)
- Entry Fee: ₹20 for adults, ₹10 for children
- Vehicle Entry: Additional charges for cars/bikes
- Photography: Allowed, professional photography requires permission
- Time Required: 2-4 hours for complete visit

Activities:
- Wildlife Safari: Vehicle and walking safaris
- Bird Watching: Early morning is best time
- Photography: Wildlife and nature photography
- Nature Walks: Guided and self-guided walks
- Educational Tours: School and college groups

SHAURYA SMARAK (PRIDE OF INDIA PARK)
====================================

Overview:
Shaurya Smarak is a memorial park dedicated to the martyrs and heroes of the Indian armed forces. It combines patriotic significance with recreational facilities.

Location:
- Address: Arera Hills, Bhopal
- Area: Large landscaped complex
- Theme: Military and patriotic
- Established: Modern memorial park

Features:

**Memorial Elements:**
- War Memorial: Dedicated to fallen soldiers
- Aircraft Display: Decommissioned military aircraft
- Tank Display: Historical military tanks and vehicles
- Memorial Wall: Names of martyrs and heroes
- Eternal Flame: Symbolic flame for remembrance

**Recreational Facilities:**
- Gardens: Well-maintained landscaped areas
- Walking Tracks: Paved paths for visitors
- Children's Play Area: Safe play zones for kids
- Amphitheater: For cultural programs and events
- Food Court: Refreshment facilities

**Educational Elements:**
- Museum: Military history and information
- Information Boards: Educational displays
- Documentary Screening: Regular shows about armed forces
- Library: Books on military history and patriotism
- Souvenir Shop: Military-themed items

Visiting Details:
- Timings: 10:00 AM - 8:00 PM
- Entry Fee: Nominal charges
- Parking: Adequate parking facilities
- Best Time: Evening hours for pleasant weather
- Special Events: Republic Day and Independence Day programs

SEASONAL HIGHLIGHTS

Winter Season (November - February):
- Pleasant weather for all outdoor activities
- Peak time for park visits

- Comfortable for long walks and picnics
- Ideal for family outings

Summer Season (March - June):
- Early morning and late evening visits recommended
- Water activities more popular
- Shaded areas preferred
- Indoor facilities more utilized
- Shorter outdoor activity periods

Monsoon Season (July - October):
- Lush green landscapes
- Beautiful natural scenery
- Limited outdoor activities
- Best for photography enthusiasts
- Peaceful and less crowded
