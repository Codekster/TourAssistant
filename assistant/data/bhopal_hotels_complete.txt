## Accommodation by Area

### Near Upper Lake (Tourist Hub)
- **Best For**: Sightseeing, scenic views
- **Hotels**: Jehan Numa Palace, Lake View Ashok
- **Pros**: Tourist attractions nearby, scenic location
- **Cons**: Slightly higher prices

### Railway Station Area
- **Best For**: Transit, budget travelers
- **Hotels**: Hotel Rajhans Regent, Hotel Ranjit
- **Pros**: Convenient for train travel, many options
- **Cons**: Crowded area, noise

### M.P. Nagar/New Market
- **Best For**: Shopping, business
- **Hotels**: Hotel Palash Residency, Hotel Shreemaya Celebrity
- **Pros**: Shopping nearby, modern area
- **Cons**: Less scenic, commercial area

### Arera Colony
- **Best For**: Upscale stay, modern amenities
- **Hotels**: Courtyard by Marriott
- **Pros**: Upscale area, modern facilities
- **Cons**: Higher prices, less local character

## Booking Tips

### Best Time to Book
- **Peak Season**: October-March (book 2-3 weeks ahead)
- **Off Season**: April-September (last minute deals available)
- **Festivals**: Book 1 month ahead during Diwali, Dussehra

### Online Platforms
- **Hotel Websites**: Direct booking, best rates guarantee
- **Travel Portals**: MakeMyTrip, Goibibo, Booking.com
- **Government**: MP Tourism for government properties

### Price Negotiation
- **Walk-in**: Possible during off-season
- **Group Bookings**: Discounts for 5+ rooms
- **Extended Stays**: Weekly/monthly rates available

This comprehensive accommodation guide helps tourists choose the right hotel based on their budget, preferences, and location requirements in Bhopal.
