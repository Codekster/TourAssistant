BHOPAL MODERN ATTRACTIONS, SHOPPING AND ENTERTAINMENT

DB CITY MALL
=============

Overview:
DB City Mall is Bhopal's premier shopping and entertainment destination. It's one of the largest malls in Central India, offering a complete shopping, dining, and entertainment experience.

Location:
- Address: Zone 1, MP Nagar, Bhopal, Madhya Pradesh 462011
- Area: 1.3 million square feet
- Opened: 2010
- Parking: Multi-level parking for 2000+ vehicles

Shopping:
- Floors: 4 levels plus basement
- Brands: 200+ national and international brands
- Categories: Fashion, electronics, books, groceries, lifestyle

Major Stores:
- Reliance Digital (electronics)
- Westside (fashion)
- Central (department store)
- Lifestyle (fashion and home)
- Spencer's (groceries)
- Crossword (books)

Fashion Brands:
- Zara, H&M, Forever 21
- Arrow, Van Heusen, Peter England
- W, Global Desi, AND
- Nike, Adidas, Puma
- Levi's, Wrangler, Pepe Jeans

Entertainment:
- PVR Cinemas: 8-screen multiplex with latest movies
- Gaming Zone: Video games and arcade
- Kids Play Area: Entertainment for children
- Food Court: 20+ food outlets

Dining Options:

**Food Court (Level 3):**
- McDonald's, KFC, Pizza Hut
- Domino's, Subway, Burger King
- Chinese, South Indian, North Indian cuisines
- Ice cream parlors and dessert shops

**Restaurants:**
- Barbeque Nation (buffet restaurant)
- TGI Friday's (American cuisine)
- Cafe Coffee Day, Starbucks
- Local and regional cuisine options

**Fast Food:**
- Multiple quick-service restaurants
- Street food court with local flavors
- Juice bars and snack counters

Facilities:
- ATMs and banking services
- Customer service desk
- Wheelchair accessibility
- Baby care rooms
- Free Wi-Fi throughout the mall

Timings:
- Shopping: 11:00 AM - 10:00 PM (All days)
- Restaurants: 11:00 AM - 11:00 PM
- Cinema: Show timings vary (usually 9:00 AM - 11:30 PM)

MP NAGAR SHOPPING AREA
=======================

Overview:
MP Nagar is Bhopal's main commercial and shopping district. It's divided into different zones, with Zone 1 and Zone 2 being the most popular for shopping.

Zone 1:
- DB City Mall location
- High-end brand stores
- Restaurants and cafes
- Banking and business centers

Zone 2:
- Local market atmosphere
- Street shopping options
- Traditional and modern stores
- Food stalls and restaurants

Shopping Categories:

**Fashion and Clothing:**
- Branded showrooms
- Local designer stores
- Traditional wear shops
- Western wear outlets

**Electronics:**
- Mobile phone stores
- Computer and laptop dealers
- Home appliance showrooms
- Camera and accessories shops

**Jewelry:**
- Gold and silver jewelry shops
- Artificial jewelry stores
- Precious stone dealers
- Traditional ornament shops

**Books and Stationery:**
- Bookstores with academic and general books
- Stationery shops
- Art and craft supplies
- Educational material stores

CHOWK BAZAR (OLD BHOPAL SHOPPING)
==================================

Overview:
Chowk Bazar is the heart of Old Bhopal's shopping area, known for its traditional atmosphere, local crafts, and authentic Bhopal specialties.

Location:
- Old Bhopal area
- Near Taj-ul-Masajid
- Traditional market setting

Shopping Specialties:

**Traditional Crafts:**
- Bhopal's famous beadwork (Moti ka kaam)
- Zardozi embroidery work
- Traditional textiles
- Handicrafts and souvenirs

**Jewelry:**
- Traditional jewelry designs
- Silver ornaments
- Kundan and meenakari work
- Antique jewelry pieces

**Textiles:**
- Traditional fabrics
- Dress materials
- Sarees and lehengas
- Men's traditional wear

**Food Items:**
- Bhopal's famous street food ingredients
- Spices and condiments
- Dry fruits and nuts
- Traditional sweets and snacks
