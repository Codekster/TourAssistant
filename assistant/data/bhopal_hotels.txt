Bhopal Hotels and Accommodation Guide

Luxury Hotels:

Jehan Numa Palace Hotel:
- Heritage palace hotel
- Located near Upper Lake
- Swimming pool, spa, multiple restaurants
- Price range: Rs. 8,000 - Rs. 15,000 per night
- Contact: +91-755-2661100

Hotel Lake View Ashok:
- Government-run hotel with lake views
- Located on Shyamla Hills
- Conference facilities available
- Price range: Rs. 4,000 - Rs. 8,000 per night
- Good for business travelers

Mid-Range Hotels:

Hotel Palash Residency:
- Located in New Market area
- Clean rooms with AC
- Restaurant and room service
- Price range: Rs. 2,500 - Rs. 4,000 per night
- Good for families

Noor-Us-Sabah Palace:
- Heritage hotel experience
- Traditional Nawabi architecture
- Cultural programs in evenings
- Price range: Rs. 3,000 - Rs. 6,000 per night

Budget Options:

Hotel Rajhans:
- Near railway station
- Basic amenities
- Price range: Rs. 1,000 - Rs. 2,000 per night
- Good for budget travelers

Youth Hostel:
- Government-run accommodation
- Dormitory and private rooms
- Price range: Rs. 300 - Rs. 800 per night
- Advance booking recommended

Areas to Stay:

New Market Area:
- Central location
- Good shopping and dining options
- Close to major attractions

Shyamla Hills:
- Upscale area
- Government offices nearby
- Quiet and peaceful

MP Nagar:
- Commercial hub
- Good connectivity
- Many restaurants and shops

Booking Tips:
- Book in advance during festival seasons
- October to March is peak season
- Many hotels offer package deals
- Check for government ID discounts
