# Bhopal Food & Restaurant Guide

## Iconic Bhopal Cuisine

### Traditional Bhopal Dishes

#### Bhopal Biryani
- **Style**: Nawabi style with tender mutton or chicken
- **Characteristics**: Fragrant basmati rice, slow-cooked, saffron-infused
- **Best Places**: Jameel Hotel, Hakeem Hotel, Bapu Ki Kutia
- **Price Range**: ₹150-400 per plate
- **Specialty**: Served with raita, shorba, and boiled eggs

#### Bhopali Paan
- **Type**: Betel leaf preparation with various fillings
- **Famous Shops**: Gopal Paan Palace (since 1953), Hari Om Paan
- **Varieties**: Sweet paan, tobacco paan, special masala paan
- **Price**: ₹20-100 per paan
- **Location**: Chowk area, New Market

#### Bhopali Seekh Kebab
- **Style**: Spiced ground meat on skewers
- **Preparation**: Clay oven (tandoor) cooked
- **Best Places**: Jameel Hotel, Kwality Restaurant
- **Accompaniments**: Mint chutney, onion rings, naan
- **Price**: ₹200-350 for 6 pieces

#### Bhopali Korma
- **Type**: Rich, creamy curry with meat or vegetables
- **Flavor**: Mild, aromatic with cashews and cream
- **Best Places**: Bapu Ki Kutia, Sagar Gaire
- **Served With**: Naan, rice, roomali roti
- **Price**: ₹250-450 per dish

#### Bhutte Ka Kees
- **Type**: Grated corn curry, popular street food
- **Preparation**: Corn cooked in milk and spices
- **Season**: Monsoon (July-September) when corn is fresh
- **Best Places**: Street vendors near Upper Lake, Chowk
- **Price**: ₹30-60 per plate

## Popular Local Restaurants

### Jameel Hotel
- **Location**: Jinsi, Old Bhopal
- **Famous For**: Authentic Bhopali Biryani
- **Established**: 1980s
- **Signature**: Mutton Biryani, Chicken Korma
- **Price Range**: ₹200-500 per person
- **Timings**: 11:00 AM - 11:00 PM
- **Special**: Authentic old city flavors, local favorite

### Bapu Ki Kutia
- **Location**: New Market Area
- **Cuisine**: North Indian, Mughlai
- **Famous For**: Non-vegetarian dishes, traditional recipes
- **Signature**: Mutton Korma, Chicken Biryani, Seekh Kebab
- **Price Range**: ₹300-600 per person
- **Timings**: 11:00 AM - 11:00 PM
- **Special**: Family restaurant, consistent quality

### Kwality Restaurant
- **Location**: M.P. Nagar
- **Cuisine**: North Indian, Chinese, Continental
- **Established**: 1960s (heritage restaurant)
- **Signature**: Chicken Tikka, Mutton Curry, Ice creams
- **Price Range**: ₹250-500 per person
- **Timings**: 11:00 AM - 11:00 PM
- **Special**: Vintage charm, ice cream parlor attached

### Sagar Gaire
- **Location**: Hamidia Road
- **Cuisine**: Vegetarian North Indian, South Indian
- **Famous For**: Pure vegetarian meals, South Indian breakfast
- **Signature**: Thali meals, Dosa varieties, Chole Bhature
- **Price Range**: ₹150-350 per person
- **Timings**: 7:00 AM - 10:30 PM
- **Special**: Pure vegetarian, family-friendly

### Hakeem Hotel
- **Location**: Chowk, Old Bhopal
- **Cuisine**: Mughlai, traditional Bhopali
- **Famous For**: Old-style cooking, authentic flavors
- **Signature**: Biryani, Mutton Curry, Traditional sweets
- **Price Range**: ₹200-450 per person
- **Timings**: 11:00 AM - 10:00 PM
- **Special**: Heritage recipes, old Bhopal experience

## Dining Etiquette & Tips

### Local Customs
- **Hand Washing**: Wash hands before meals
- **Eating Style**: Many locals eat with hands (traditional)
- **Spice Levels**: Ask for mild if you're not used to spicy food
- **Tipping**: 10% in restaurants, optional in street food

### Food Safety
- **Water**: Drink bottled water
- **Street Food**: Choose busy stalls with high turnover
- **Hygiene**: Look for clean preparation areas
- **Timing**: Avoid very late night street food

### Budget Planning
- **Street Food**: ₹50-150 per meal
- **Mid-range Restaurants**: ₹300-600 per person
- **Fine Dining**: ₹1,000-3,000 per person
- **Daily Food Budget**: ₹500-1,500 per person

This comprehensive food guide helps visitors explore Bhopal's rich culinary heritage, from street food to fine dining, ensuring a complete gastronomic experience.
