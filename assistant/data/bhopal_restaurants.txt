Bhopal Restaurant and Food Guide

Traditional Bhopali Cuisine:

Famous Dishes:
- Bhopali Gosht Korma: Rich mutton curry with aromatic spices
- Seekh Kebabs: Grilled minced meat skewers
- Bhopali Biryani: Fragrant rice dish with meat and spices
- Bhopali Paan: Traditional betel leaf preparation
- Poha-Jalebi: Popular breakfast combination

Top Restaurants:

Manohar Dairy & Restaurant:
- Famous for South Indian food
- Location: MP Nagar
- Specialty: Dosa, Idli, and sweets
- Price range: Rs. 200 - Rs. 500 per person
- Open: 6:00 AM to 11:00 PM

Jameel Hotel:
- Authentic Mughlai cuisine
- Location: Chowk area
- Specialty: Mutton korma and kebabs
- Price range: Rs. 300 - Rs. 800 per person
- Famous since 1960s

Kwality Restaurant:
- Multi-cuisine family restaurant
- Location: New Market
- Specialty: North Indian and Chinese
- Price range: Rs. 250 - Rs. 600 per person
- Good for families

Bapu Ki Kutia:
- Traditional vegetarian thali
- Location: TT Nagar
- Specialty: Unlimited thali meals
- Price range: Rs. 150 - Rs. 300 per person
- Popular among locals

Street Food Areas:

Chowk Bazaar:
- Traditional market area
- Best for: Kebabs, biryanis, sweets
- Evening is the best time
- Try: Jalebi, samosas, chaat

New Market:
- Modern shopping area
- Good food courts
- Best for: Fast food and snacks
- Family-friendly environment

Boat Club Area:
- Near Upper Lake
- Good for: Evening snacks
- Beautiful lake views while eating
- Try: Corn, tea, pakoras

Food Timings:
- Breakfast: 7:00 AM - 10:00 AM
- Lunch: 12:00 PM - 3:00 PM
- Dinner: 7:00 PM - 11:00 PM
- Street food: 5:00 PM onwards

Food Safety Tips:
- Drink bottled water
- Eat at busy, popular places
- Avoid raw vegetables in street food
- Try local specialties gradually
