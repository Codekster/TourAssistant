MAJOR ATTRACTIONS AND LANDMARKS

LAKES AND WATER BODIES
=======================
✓ Upper Lake (Bhojtal) - Largest artificial lake in Asia, boating, sunset views
✓ Lower Lake (Chhota Talab) - Beautiful lake with Kamla Park, boating facilities  
✓ Kerwa Dam - Popular picnic spot, water sports, fishing, 15km from city
✓ Kaliasot Dam - Serene environment, trekking, bird watching, 25km from city
✓ Motia Talab - Peaceful lake for morning walks and relaxation

HISTORICAL AND ARCHAEOLOGICAL SITES
===================================
✓ Sanchi Stupa - UNESCO World Heritage Site, Buddhist monuments, 46km from Bhopal
✓ Bhojeshwar Temple - 11th century Shiva temple, massive lingam, architectural marvel
✓ Bhimbetka Caves - UNESCO site, prehistoric rock paintings, 45km from Bhopal
✓ Udayagiri Caves - Ancient rock-cut caves, Gupta period, Hindu and Jain sculptures
✓ Raisen Fort - Medieval hill fort, panoramic views, historical significance
✓ Islamnagar - Mughal town, historical ruins, architectural remains
✓ Gyaraspur - Medieval temples, rock-cut caves, historical importance
